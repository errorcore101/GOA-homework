sedans

A sedan is a car with four doors and a separate trunk, featuring a "three-box" design with separate compartments for the engine, passengers, and cargo. Sedans are known for being versatile, offering a comfortable ride, good fuel economy, and enough space for four to five passengers, making them popular for both daily commutes and families.

SUVS

SUVs, or Sport Utility Vehicles, are a type of vehicle that blends the high ground clearance and off-road capabilities of a truck with the passenger comfort of a car. They are known for their spacious, versatile interiors, higher ride height, and rugged construction, making them suitable for both city driving and rougher terrain. SUVs come in various sizes.

luxury

Luxury cars are vehicles that go beyond basic transportation by offering superior comfort, advanced technology, premium materials, and high-end performance. They are known for high-quality interiors with features like fine leather and wood trim, sophisticated electronics, powerful engines.
